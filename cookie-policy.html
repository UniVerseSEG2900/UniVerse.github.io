Cookie Policy

Updated on November 24, 2024

1. Introduction

At UniVerse, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, improve functionality, and provide a personalized experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as enabling navigation and accessing secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how visitors use the site to help us improve functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver content and advertisements tailored to your interests.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, some features of our site may not function properly without cookies.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for updates.

For more information about our use of cookies, contact us at privacy@universe.com.
