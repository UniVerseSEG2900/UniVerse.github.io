About Us

Meet the UniVerse Team

Six university students passionate about bridging language barriers through innovative technology. Our journey began with a shared vision to create a tool that could bring people closer together, regardless of the languages they speak. Together, we combined our skills in engineering, design, and software development to create UniVerse, a device that empowers users to communicate freely across languages. Driven by curiosity and a desire to make global connections easier, we designed UniVerse to be intuitive, stylish, and accessible to all!
