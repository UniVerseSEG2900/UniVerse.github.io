Use of Cookies

Updated on November 24, 2024

Overview

At UniVerse, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Site Functionality: Cookies help us collect information about how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to improve its functionality and user experience.
  • Remember Preferences: Cookies enable us to remember your preferences, such as language settings and login details, for a more personalized experience.
  • Enhance User Experience: By storing your preferences, cookies allow us to tailor the site to your needs, such as keeping you logged in or saving items in your shopping cart.

Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or decline cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may limit some functionality of the site.
